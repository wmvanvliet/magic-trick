# Experiment protocol for the "Magic Trick" demo

This is an experiment based on the P300-oddball effect, written using PsychoPy.

Cards:

1. Ace of spades
2. Jack of clubs
3. Queen of hearts
4. King of diamonds
5. 10 of spades
6. 3 of clubs
7. 10 of hearts
8. 3 of diamonds
9. King of spades
